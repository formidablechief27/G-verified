Marketing Strategy

Marketing strategy is a plan that outlines how a business will promote and sell its products or services, targeting specific audiences and using various tactics to achieve its overall objectives.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the practice of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results, increasing visibility and attracting more organic traffic.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO involves optimizing the technical aspects of a website to improve its search engine performance, focusing on factors like site structure, code, for better visibility and user experience.

PPC (Pay Per Click)

Pay Per Click, is an online advertising strategy where advertisers pay for each click on their ads.Advertisers bid on keywords, and when users search for them, the ads are displayed.

SEM ( Search Engine Marketing )

SEM is an online advertising method that increases website visibility through paid strategies like Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising, aiming to drive targeted traffic and improve online presence.

Content Strategy & Marketing

Content strategy and marketing involve planning and creating relevant content to engage a target audience. It plays a crucial role in establishing a strong online presence and fostering customer relationships.

Messaging

Messaging involves strategically communicating concise and impactful information to achieve specific goals, resonating with the audience for desired actions in marketing or interpersonal communication.

Content Writing

Content writing is the creation of written material for online or print platforms. It involves crafting engaging and informative content to attract specific audience. It incorporates SEO principles for improved visibility .

CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization)

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) aims to improve the percentage of website visitors who take desired actions, such as making a purchase, through strategic analysis and testing.

Web Design

Web design involves creating visually appealing and user-friendly layouts for websites, combining graphic elements for effective communication and a positive user experience.

Web Development

Web development involves building and maintaining websites or web applications, covering both user interface design and server-side programming for functionality and interactivity.

Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing utilizes platforms to promote products, build brand awareness, and engage audiences through content creation and advertising to achieve business goals and enhance online presence.

Logo Design & Branding

Logo design and branding involve creating a distinctive visual identity for a company. It includes designing a unique logo and establishing a cohesive brand image to communicate the business's values.

Analytics & Attribution

Analytics and attribution in marketing refer to tracking and analyzing data to measure campaign performance. Attribution models help identify the channels contributing to conversions.
